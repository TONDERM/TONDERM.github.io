Hello and welcome to my site! My name is Mike Tonder and I live in Traverse City, Michigan. I'm looking for work in the field of web design. My former field was with 3d graphics (FX, Games Ect.) but now I'm venturing into the world of website creation. I'm a front-end specialist and like to be creative and fun with what I do.

I like to push myself, trying new and creative ideas I haven't done before. While this can add to something being a more difficult task, it ultimately proves more rewarding, providing a better learning experience and creation of a better product.

I still need one more class before I can say I graduated. It's an internship so I need to be hired somewhere that will be able to provide that credit.

I took 2 years of IT Infrastrucure but found myself needing a creative outlet in some way. My interest and enjoyment for IT was not as it should be to really want to continue forward as a career. With this in mind, I decided to change my major to Web Development. Switching felt like the best choice of action, even though I had to start over with classes. In the end, it was the right decision.

I really enjoy doing front-end graphics and design. When you find yourself experimenting, exploring and working on stuff unrelated to school projects, you know you've found the right path. I'm not as stressed as when working in the 3D/Gaming industry and it still provides me with doing something creative, to feed that artistic need.
