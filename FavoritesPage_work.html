My Favorites

The Sampler Platter

These are some of my favorites from Calvin and Hobbes. The "Art of Snow" is a collection of strips where Calvin builds his snow creations. Personally, these hold the funniest moments of the series. The other collections are just as good though, showing the creativity of Calvin's point of view mixed with the reality of being a little kid. It's why I love the series so much. It also has a lot of heart, great story arcs and a lot of memerable and interesting characters.
