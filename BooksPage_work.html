Calvin and Hobbes book collections

Released April 1987. Collection covering strips from first strip on Nov 18, 1985 to Aug 17, 1986. Original content: Foreword by Garry Trudeau.

Released April 1988. Collection covering strips from Aug 18, 1986 to May 22, 1987. Original content: Foreword by Pat Oliphant.

Released March 1989. Collection covering strips from May 24, 1987 to Feb 21, 1988. Original content: The Yukon Song.

Released September 1989. Collection covering from May 24, 1987 to July 30, 1989 (Sunday strips only). Original content: ten-page story "Spaceman Spiff: Interplanetary Explorer Extraordinaire!"

Released March 1990. Collection covering strips from Feb 22, 1988 to Dec 4, 1988, minus Nov 27-30, 1988.

Released April 1991. Collection covering strips from Dec 5, 1988 to Sept 10, 1989, plus Nov 27-30, 1988.

Released October 1991. Collection covering strips from Sept 11, 1989 to Jul 7, 1990, minus June 11-16 and 18-23, and July 1 and 8, 1990.

Released April 1992. Collection covering strips from Jul 8, 1990 to Apr 10, 1991, plus June 11-16 and 18-23, and July 1 and 8, 1990.

Released October 1993. Collection covering strips from Apr 11, 1991 to Nov 1, 1992, excluding May 5, 1991 to Feb 1, 1992 due to repeats.

Released October 1994. Collection covering strips from Nov 2, 1992 to Aug 29, 1993.

Released October 1995. Various strips ranging from 1985 to 1995. Original content: Essays by Watterson and annotations on individual strips.

Released March 1996. Collection covering strips from Aug 30, 1993 to Apr 8, 1995, minus March 20 - April 2, 1995, and April 3 to December 31, 1994 due to repeats.

Released October 1996. Final collection covering strips from April 9, 1995 to the last strip on Dec 31, 1995, plus March 20 - April 2, 1995.
