The Art of Snow
Calvin's "snowmen" are probably my favorite part of the series. While starting out some what simple, it has evolved into a very clear window into Calvin's mind. Most of these would be impossible to make in reality but for Calvin, it's just another day outside during winter. Aside from the improbability of such creations (plus the fact there's never a shortage of snow), the strange thing is that his parents never react to the incredible skill, effort and imagination it takes for Calvin to create them. They react as if this is just another thing to put up with. If he put as much effort into school as he does his creations, he'd probably be a genius!
