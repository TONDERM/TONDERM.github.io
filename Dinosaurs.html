When Dinosaurs Roam
Like Spaceman Spiff, Dinosaurs provide Calvin with another reality. Depending on what he's daydreaming about, Calvin is either himself interacting or he's a Dinosaur. Of course he is an apex preditor when he imagines himself as one, usually the Calvinosaurus, which often provides a humurous juxtiposition of his imagination and reality. Calvin usually daydreams himself amoung Dinasaurs when he's at school or some other place he'd rather not be. When out and about, he imagines himself as Calvinosaurus or some other apex preditor. Hunting for prey that are usually his parents or Susie.
